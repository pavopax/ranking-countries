Where would you like to live?

Create your own custom ranking of the world's countries.

Or, explore the underlying data and discover some insights.

We're all familiar with various rankings of the world's universities. Recently, however, US News came out with a ranking of the world's countries, which was based on proprietary survey data.

In contrast, the ranking you can build here uses 29 publicly available metrics from The World Bank, The World Economic Forum (Global Competitiveness Index), and the WIN/Gallup International End of the Year Survey.

Read methodological details and see the code on this Github repository.
